Common Questions

Straight answers.

No fluff. If you have a question not listed here, just reach out.

What can my VA actually do?

Whatever your business needs most. Common functions include social media management, bookkeeping, CRM and email management, scheduling, invoice follow-up, data entry, and general administrative support. We match the VA to your specific needs during onboarding.

How fast can I get a VA?

Often same day. We have an established sourcing network and move quickly once we understand what you need. In most cases you'll have a qualified candidate the same day you decide to move forward.

What if my VA isn't performing?

That's exactly why we're here. During Phase 1 we manage performance and step in if something isn't working. If a replacement is needed, we handle it — at no additional cost to you.

When do I transition from Phase 1 to Phase 2?

Typically between months 3 and 6, once your VA is running smoothly and your systems are documented. The timing depends on your situation — we'll discuss it together and make the transition when it makes sense for your business.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We earn your continued business by delivering results, not by locking you in. You can cancel at any time.

What if I need more than 2 calls a month on the retainer?

If you need additional support beyond what's included in the retainer, we're available at $50/hour. If you find yourself needing frequent help, it might make sense to discuss adding a second VA or expanding the scope — we'll talk through the options honestly.

Let's talk about what your business needs. No pressure, no obligation — just a straight conversation about whether this is the right fit.
